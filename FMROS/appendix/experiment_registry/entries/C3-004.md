# C3-004 — M1 fidelity replay of composite trades

Program: P7 · State: closed · **Verdict: IMMATERIAL**

## Question (frozen)
Does the 15m pessimistic same-bar tie-break materially bias the
composite's reported expectancy? MATERIAL if |correction| ≥ 0.02R on
either sample.

## Prerequisite discovered and fixed
Trade records did not persist entry/TP/SL levels (schema defect). Fixed
(`simulate_composite` now emits entry/tp/sl/lev); both simulations
re-run deterministically; anchor assertions (n=414/exp +0.1279; n=334/
exp +0.0788) passed, confirming reproducibility.

## Result (`research/cycle3/results/c3_004_m1_replay.json`)
Of 205 (pilot) + 155 (confirmation) SL exits replayed on minute bars,
**0 flip to TP**. Correction = 0.000R on both samples. Wide barriers
(TP 3×ATR / SL 2×ATR) make same-bar double-touches practically
impossible; minute-level replay stays pessimistic on same-minute ties,
so this is a bound, and it is zero.

## Lessons
The composite's numbers are free of tie-break bias; the fidelity
priority shifts to the fill model's cost assumption (C3-002), not bar
resolution.
