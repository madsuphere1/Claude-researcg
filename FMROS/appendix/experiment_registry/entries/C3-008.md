# C3-008 — Expanding vs rolling training windows, head-to-head on 2027+ data (P10)

Program: P10 · State: PRE-REGISTERED (standing, calendar-blocked)

## Hypothesis (operator-proposed, 2026-07-10)
Recent structural change makes long history harmful: X1 driven by
rolling-window models outperforms X1 driven by expanding-window models
on genuinely new data. In the operator's words: "if moving window works
then I'm right."

## Relation to H-I1 (why this is not a re-litigation)
H-I1 REFUTED rolling windows at the AUC level across 2014-2026 overall
(expanding 0.529 vs rolling5 0.523 vs rolling3 0.519 mean AUC). Two
things justify a standing successor without violating Constitution §1.6:
(1) NEW DATA — this test consumes only 2027+ observations, which no
model or analysis has ever seen; (2) a declared mechanism sliver —
rolling5 won the recorded AUC head-to-head in 2024 and 2026 (2 of the
last 3 years), consistent with (though far from proving) a recent-era
structural change. An exploratory screen on the even-year pilot slice
(`research/cycle3/screen_rolling_x1.py`, results
`research/cycle3/results/screen_rolling_x1.json`) is hypothesis-
generating context only and carries no confirmatory weight.

## Design (frozen)
Each new full calendar year Y >= 2027, as HistData publishes:
* Run the frozen X1 spec (registry C3-001, verbatim) twice on year Y:
  variant E = expanding-window models (house protocol, train 2010..Y-1);
  variant R = rolling 5-year models (train Y-5..Y-1, the stronger
  rolling variant from H-I1; W is FROZEN at 5 — no window search).
  Classifier window is the only difference; HMM gate, thresholds
  protocol, execution, and risk rules identical.
* Record for each variant: n_trades, net expectancy (1.25bp), win rate,
  per-regime dissection.

## DECISION RULE (frozen before any test data exists)
Evaluate after THREE full new years (2027, 2028, 2029):
* ACCEPT-ROLLING (house rule changes to rolling5) if R's pooled net
  expectancy exceeds E's by > +0.02R AND R wins the annual head-to-head
  in >= 2 of 3 years.
* RETAIN-EXPANDING otherwise.
No interim verdicts; annual results are recorded but not acted on.
The window length may not be tuned on interim results; any new window
proposal is a new registry entry requiring its own mechanism.

## Status
Pre-registered 2026-07-10. First evaluable year: 2027 (data accrues
~26k bars/year). Blocked on calendar, not on operator or design.
