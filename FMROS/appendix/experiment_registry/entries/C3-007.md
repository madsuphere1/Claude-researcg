# C3-007 — GDELT textual/media-tone signal (P8, successor to C3-003)

Program: P8 · State: PRE-REGISTERED → (updated on verdict)

## Hypothesis
Automated NLP-derived textual signal from global news coverage (GDELT
Global Knowledge Graph tone and theme tags) carries information about
XAUUSD 15m barrier outcomes that price alone and lagged numeric macro
series (C3-003) both lack; adding a small frozen tone/theme feature
family raises walk-forward AUC by >= +0.003.

## Mechanism and relation to C3-003
C3-003 tested *numeric* daily macro levels (yields, dollar, VIX, oil)
2-day-lagged and found no incremental value — REJECTED, mean uplift
-0.0087. The candidate mechanism here is different in kind, not degree:
media tone/theme coverage can shift on a narrative before it shows up in
a lagging macro print (e.g. hawkish-Fed rhetoric building across days of
coverage ahead of the next DFII10 print, or an armed-conflict/safe-haven
narrative accumulating before flows move the DXY). This is the
TradingAgents-style claim (textual reasoning over numeric macro state)
operationalized in the one way that avoids a serious contamination risk
[see Threat 2 below]: GDELT's GKG is *already* an automated NLP pipeline
(tone scoring + theme tagging) run at publication time, not a generative
LLM asked to retrospectively "reason" about historical news.

## Two threats declared BEFORE running (mechanism-before-mining)
1. **Same frequency-mismatch threat that killed C3-003.** Narrative/media
   attention plausibly still resolves over hours-to-days, not the 4-hour
   barrier horizon used here. This hypothesis may fail for the identical
   structural reason C3-003 did. Declaring this now so a null result is
   not spun as a surprise.
2. **Generative-LLM contamination (why this is NOT a TradingAgents-style
   backtest).** Asking a generative LLM to "reason" about historical news
   text during a backtest risks the model's own pretraining knowledge of
   what happened *after* that news item leaking into its "analysis" —
   silent lookahead bias that inflates walk-forward AUC without being
   caught by the standard leakage screens (the leak is in the model's
   weights, not the joined dataframe). This experiment uses only GDELT's
   deterministic, publication-time tone/theme scores — no generative
   model is called — specifically to avoid this failure mode. A true
   generative-LLM-reasoning variant is filed to the queue as
   [HYPOTHESIS, P8] but constrained to **forward/paper evaluation only**,
   never a historical backtest, until a contamination-safe design exists.

## Data source and coverage (documented limitation)
GDELT GKG 2.1, `data.gdeltproject.org/gdeltv2/*.gkg.csv.zip`, native
15-minute granularity from 2015-02-18 onward (public, free, confirmed
reachable). **No coverage before 2015-02-18** — a real data-availability
limit, not engineered around. One weekly snapshot file (Monday
12:00:00 UTC, or nearest available slot) is sampled per week from
2015-02-23 through the present as a bandwidth-bounded proxy for
continuous coverage; this is coarser than C3-003's daily macro cadence
and is declared as a limitation of this pilot, not hidden.

## Declared feature family (6 features, frozen)
From each sampled weekly GKG file: mean V2Tone (field 1, "avg_tone"),
mean V2Tone field 3 ("tone_negativity"), and the share of records whose
V2Themes contains each of: `ECON_INFLATION`, `EPU_POLICY`, `ECON_DEBT`,
`ARMEDCONFLICT`. All six z-scored on a 52-week trailing window, shifted
2 business days (same availability conservatism as C3-003), forward-
filled onto 15m bars with a 10-day staleness cap (wider than C3-003's
5-day cap because the underlying sampling is weekly, not daily). Bars
before 2015-02-23 and any bar past the staleness cap: all six features
NaN (LightGBM native NaN handling, consistent with existing features).

## Multiplicity universe
This 6-feature family, tested once as a block. No per-feature selection
before the block test; per-feature gain-share inspection is diagnostic
only, exactly as in C3-003.

## Design
Target y_tp_long; folds 2018 / 2022 / 2026 (house model-improvement
folds, expanding window from 2010, purge 96 bars) — identical to C3-003
for direct comparability; LightGBM house params (identical to C3-003);
baseline = current 205 features (NOT including the already-rejected
C3-003 macro block, to isolate the textual family's incremental value
cleanly); treatment = baseline + 6 GDELT features.

## DECISION RULE (frozen before run, identical structure to C3-003)
ACCEPT if mean AUC uplift >= +0.003 AND uplift negative in <= 1 of 3
folds; REJECT otherwise. Secondary diagnostics (non-gating): GDELT
features' gain share; per-fold coverage (share of bars with non-NaN
features, expected ~0% for 2010-2014 training data, high for
2015+ years).
