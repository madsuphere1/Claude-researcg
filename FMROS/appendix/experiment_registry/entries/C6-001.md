# C6-001 — Metric structure: hierarchy, redundancy, combinations (P10)

Program: P10 · State: CLOSED · Supersedes C5-001 informativeness ranking

## Motivation
Operator rejected C5-001's flat-column metric ranking as a fallacy:
metrics are a coupled algebraic system (PV=nRT analogy), not independent
axes, and C5 never tested combinations or validated forward claims OOS.

## Method
No retraining; five cached streams. (1) verify algebraic identities;
(2) correlation clustering + PCA on 155 interval cells for effective
dimensionality; (3) declare primitive/derived hierarchy; (4) OOS
combination search — all singles + pairs of 9 primitives, leave-one-out
CV R², 500-draw permutation null re-running the full best-of search on
shuffled targets. Script `research/cycle6/metric_structure.py`.

## Results (`research/cycle6/results/c6_metric_structure.json`)
* Identities exact: expectancy=p·W+(1−p)·L (err 9e-5); omega≡profit_
  factor; calmar≡recovery_factor (literal duplicate columns in C5).
* Effective dimensionality: 28 metrics → 6 axes (90% var), first PC =
  50.8%. Eleven "return" metrics form ONE |ρ|≥0.90 cluster
  {expectancy, sharpe, sortino, PF, omega, calmar, recovery, win_rate,
  total_R, downside_dev, skew}. Independent informative axes ≈ 4 (return,
  drawdown, dispersion, streak/shape); tail + trade-geometry axes are
  barrier-pinned (blind).
* Combination search: best single OOS R²=+0.006 (max_consec_losses),
  best pair +0.002 (std_R+max_consec_losses, worse than single),
  permutation p=0.284 → NOT distinguishable from noise.

## Verdict
CLOSED. Two outcomes: (1) C5-001's "HELPS" informativeness ranking is
**WITHDRAWN** — it was in-sample ρ on redundant columns; OOS+permutation
kills it (cascade per Constitution §5.7). (2) New standing doctrine
recorded in `report/METHODOLOGY.md` §7: choose metrics by independent
axis, drop barrier-pinned metrics, analyse primitives, and require
OOS-CV + permutation null for any forward-predictive claim.

## Lessons
* In-sample metric correlation across a redundant, high-multiplicity set
  is a false-positive generator — it manufactured C5's fake signal.
* On this instrument, no metric or linear pair forecasts forward
  performance beyond chance (n=42, low power — absence of evidence, not
  proof of absence). Nonlinear/higher-order combinations untestable at
  this sample size; deferred to 2027+ (C3-008) under the frozen §7
  protocol.
