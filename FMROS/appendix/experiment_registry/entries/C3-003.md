# C3-003 — Cross-asset macro conditioning (P8)

Program: P8 · State: PRE-REGISTERED → (updated on verdict)

## Hypothesis
Daily macro state — real yields (DFII10), broad dollar (DTWEXBGS), VIX,
WTI — carries information about XAUUSD 15m barrier outcomes that the
price-only feature set lacks; adding a small lagged macro family raises
walk-forward AUC by ≥ +0.003.

## Mechanism
Gold's discounted-cash-flow-free valuation makes real yields its
canonical macro anchor and the dollar its denominator; portfolio flows
respond to both with persistence measured in days-weeks — slow enough to
survive a conservative 2-business-day availability lag, fast enough to
condition intraday barrier odds. VIX/oil proxy risk-off and inflation
channels. [ESTABLISHED as macro correlations; the *incremental intraday
predictive value* is the untested claim.]

## Declared feature family (12 features, frozen)
For each series s ∈ {DFII10, DTWEXBGS, VIXCLS, DCOILWTICO}:
level z-score (252d), 5d change, 21d change — computed on daily data,
**shifted 2 business days** (availability conservatism), forward-filled
onto 15m bars with 5-day staleness cap.

## Multiplicity universe
This 12-feature family, tested once as a block. No per-feature selection
before the block test; importance inspection is diagnostic only.

## Design
Target y_tp_long; folds 2018 / 2022 / 2026 (house model-improvement
folds, expanding window, purge 96); LightGBM house params; baseline =
current 205 features; treatment = baseline + 12 macro.

## DECISION RULE (frozen before run)
ACCEPT if mean AUC uplift ≥ +0.003 AND uplift negative in ≤ 1 of 3 folds;
REJECT otherwise. Secondary diagnostics (non-gating): macro features'
gain share; uplift on y_tp_short.
