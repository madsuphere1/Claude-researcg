## What the EA is
Long-ONLY momentum trend-follower on XAUUSD M15. Entry = 5-condition
trend score ≥0.5 (RSI band, MACD>signal, price>SMA20 & EMA12>EMA26,
ADX>25, price>BB-mid). SL −2%, TP +5% (2.5R), break-even at +1%,
trailing from +2%, one position, London hours, prop guardrails
(2 consec-loss halt, daily equity stop). No sell logic.

## Verdict
It is a REAL, positive, risk-managed strategy — but its edge is
**beta (long gold), not alpha (timing)**. Its P&L is a levered,
stop-protected bet that gold rises. The disciplined stops do genuine
work: they compressed a −28% gold year (2013) to −5.7% and compounded
the up years, giving +34.8%/−14.4% over 16 years (~1.9%/yr). But it
loses in every sustained gold decline and cannot profit from down moves
(no short side).

## Why it "works" where the 205-feature alpha research "failed"
Different objective, and one real structural insight:
* **Objective**: C1-C6 hunted short-horizon *directional alpha* net of
  tight-barrier cost (near zero, by construction). This EA harvests
  *secular beta* with wide stops — not a contradiction, a different game.
* **Cost structure (the insight I missed)**: cost-in-R = spread /
  SL_distance. This EA's SL is 2% (~$40 at $2000); a 2-4bp spread is
  ~0.01-0.02R per trade — negligible. My composites used ~1×ATR stops
  (~0.2%), making cost-in-R 10-20× larger and fatal. **Wide-stop
  trend-following is cost-robust; tight-barrier direction is cost-
  fragile.** This is a genuine lesson for P5/P6.

## Real risks (honest)
1. Regime-dependent: needs gold trending up. 2013/2015/2026 lose.
2. Prop-challenge timing: a short evaluation window in a flat/down gold
   month (2013, 2015) would likely breach drawdown/target and fail;
   2024-2025 would pass easily. Pass/fail is largely gold's regime, not
   the strategy.
3. Replication is gross of spread, M15 intrabar (not M1), session filter
   on data-hour (broker server tz may differ). Numbers approximate;
   the regime-dependence is robust to all three.

## Lessons carried
* A positive long-biased beta harvester is a legitimate product and was
  wrongly excluded by framing the whole program around market-neutral
  15m alpha. Filed: P1 hypothesis — add a symmetric short side + an
  explicit trend/regime filter and measure alpha vs pure gold beta
  (does the timing add anything beyond buy-and-hold-with-stops?).
* Wide-stop geometry as a cost-mitigation lever — revisit P5.
