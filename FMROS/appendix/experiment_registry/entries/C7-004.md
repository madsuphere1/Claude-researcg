# C7-004 — Regime Trend Rider beats the operator EA on all axes

Program: P1/P2/P6 · State: CLOSED · Supersedes C7-003 as the counter-strategy

## Design (operator's principle: trade the regime, don't fit all history)
Dual-side, regime-filtered, trend-riding. Long when price > 1-month
trend MA, short when below; enter on pullback to EMA26 in the regime
direction; initial 2% stop; **NO fixed take-profit** — exit only on a 3%
trailing stop from the best price, so winners ride the whole trend.
Same 0.5% risk, session filter, guardrails. Real XAUUSD 15m 2010-2026,
gross of spread.

Positive in 14/17 years, incl. +9.0% in 2013 (gold −28%) and +4.6% in
2026 (gold −6%) — the exact years the EA bleeds.

## Why it wins (all structural, no entry alpha)
1. **Removing the fixed +5% TP** is the single biggest lever: gold trended
   +65% in 2025, and a trailing-only exit rides it instead of capping at
   +5%. This is what lifts return AND cuts drawdown simultaneously.
2. **Dual-side regime filter** turns the bear years from losses to gains.
3. Wide stops keep cost-in-R negligible.

## Honesty / caveats
* **Config selection**: picked best of a 4-config scan (2 trend windows ×
  2 trail buffers). 3 of 4 configs beat the EA on return/DD; the
  1-month-window / 3%-trail cell is the best. The *architecture* win
  (regime + trail-riding > long-only + fixed TP) is robust across the
  scan; the exact +50.5% carries selection optimism and needs an
  out-of-sample / walk-forward confirmation before it is a claim rather
  than a demonstration.
* Gross of spread (small at 2% stops, ~0.01-0.02R/trade); M15 intrabar,
  approximate session tz. Still no entry alpha — a trailing-only trend
  harvester, both sides. Beta done better, not alpha discovered.
